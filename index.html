About Me

I am a freelance web developer with over 5 years experience of building web-applications for large corporate and enterprise clients.

Since starting out life coding on the LAMP stack, I have, since early 2008, specialised in Javascript development, becoming fluent in a number of frameworks and libraries as well as developing clients' bespoke DOM and UI libraries written in native Javascript.

Since mid-2011 I have been focussed on building enterprise software based on BackboneJS, and in the course of my work have made (modest) contributions back into the Backbone project as well as other major open-source projects including Modernizr and MediaElement.js

Over the course of my career, I have worked for a number of large, high-profile end-clients, including Barclays, BSkyB, HSBC, JWT, Lloyds TSB, Marks & Spencer, O2, RBS Group, Santander, Unilever and Virgin Atlantic
